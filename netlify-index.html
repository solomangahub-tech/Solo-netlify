The Story

Origin · Lore · Universe
◆ The Manhwa That Changed Everything

From E-Rank
to Shadow Monarch

In a world forever altered by the sudden appearance of dungeons — gates connecting Earth to monster-filled realms — only those who have "Awakened" as Hunters can enter and fight. Sung Jin-Woo is ranked at E-class, the absolute bottom. Weak, underpaid, and barely surviving each raid.

Then a catastrophic double dungeon raid nearly kills his entire party. Trapped in a secret second chamber designed as a deadly trial, Jin-Woo survives through sheer will — and wakes to a blue screen only he can see. The System has chosen him.

Unlike any other hunter in history, Jin-Woo levels up. He grinds alone, in secret, growing at a pace no one thought possible. The man the world wrote off as humanity's weakest begins a journey that ends with him commanding legions of shadows and confronting beings that predate humanity itself.

Created by Chugong and illustrated by DUBU (Redice Studio), Solo Leveling became the defining manhwa of its generation, serialized on Kakao Page from 2018 to 2021.

FAQ

Quick Answers
Where can I read Solo Leveling for free online? +
All 179 chapters of the Solo Leveling manhwa are available free at solomangahub.com. No account needed, no paywall — just click and read from Chapter 1.
How many chapters does Solo Leveling have? +
The manhwa has 179 chapters plus a prologue. The original light novel by Chugong has 270+ chapters, including side stories not adapted in the manhwa. Both are available at solomangahub.com.
Is Solo Leveling a manga or manhwa? +
Solo Leveling is technically a manhwa — a Korean comic. "Manga" is the Japanese term, but Solo Leveling is widely referred to as manga by international fans. It was originally a Korean webtoon published on Kakao Page.
Is there a Solo Leveling anime and where can I watch it? +
Yes — Solo Leveling was adapted into an anime by A-1 Pictures. Season 1 aired in early 2024, Season 2 (Arise from the Shadow) followed in 2025. Visit solomangahub.com for episode guides and streaming links.
What order should I read / watch Solo Leveling in? +
Best order: 1) Manhwa chapters 1–179 for the definitive visual story. 2) Light novel for expanded scenes. 3) Anime for a different medium experience. All available free at solomangahub.com.
Who is Sung Jin-Woo and why is he special? +
Sung Jin-Woo is the protagonist — initially the world's weakest E-rank hunter. After surviving the Double Dungeon, he becomes the sole recipient of a leveling System, growing into the Shadow Monarch, a being capable of commanding armies of the dead.
