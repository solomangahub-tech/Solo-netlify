In a world where portals to monster-filled dungeons have appeared, hunters awaken with supernatural powers to fight them. Sung Jin-Woo — ranked as the weakest E-rank hunter — finds himself trapped in a double dungeon and nearly killed.

He alone survives — and receives a mysterious interface only he can see: the System. What follows is the most gripping power fantasy in manhwa history.

Story Arcs

Follow Jin-Woo's evolution arc by arc, from a hopeless E-rank to a being beyond human comprehension.

01
◆ Prologue Arc
Awakening & The Double Dungeon

Jin-Woo is the weakest hunter alive. A disastrous double dungeon raid nearly kills him — until a mysterious quest window appears before him, visible only to him. He accepts and awakens to a power no one has ever seen.

Chapters 1–14
02
◆ Training Arc
The System & Rapid Growth

With the System guiding him like an RPG, Jin-Woo grinds dungeons alone, leveling up at a pace that defies all known logic. His stats soar past any recorded hunter, drawing suspicion and awe.

Chapters 15–53
03
◆ Jeju Island Arc
The Ant King Raid

S-rank hunters from across Korea mount a desperate assault on Jeju Island, overrun by ant-type monsters. Jin-Woo faces Beru, the Ant King — a battle that rewrites what it means to be powerful.

Chapters 54–109
04
◆ International Guild Arc
World's Attention

Word spreads of an unholy power in Korea. Top guilds from the US, China, and Europe make their moves. Jin-Woo's shadow army is tested on a global stage.

Solo Leveling FAQ

You can read all 179 chapters of the Solo Leveling manhwa free at solomangahub.com. We host the full webtoon in high-quality scans, fully translated to English, with no paywalls.
The Solo Leveling manhwa (Korean webtoon) has 179 chapters plus a prologue. The original light novel by Chugong has 270+ chapters with additional side stories not adapted in the manhwa.
Yes! Solo Leveling was adapted into an anime by A-1 Pictures in 2024. Season 1 aired from January to March 2024 and covered the early arcs. Season 2 — subtitled "Arise from the Shadow" — followed in 2025.
The recommended order is: (1) Read the manhwa/webtoon chapters 1–179 for the definitive visual story. (2) Read the light novel for expanded scenes and details. (3) Watch the anime for a different medium experience. All available at solomangahub.com.
The protagonist is Sung Jin-Woo, a Korean hunter initially ranked at E-class — the lowest possible. After surviving the "Double Dungeon" incident, he becomes the only human to receive a special leveling system and grows into the Shadow Monarch.
